⚠️ Threshold Policy

For HFT 1-Step live accounts, all positive deductions from consistency checks (daily profit, lot size consistency, trades under 60 seconds, aggregated trades, etc.) may not exceed 10% of the total-cycle profit. If the total deductions are higher than this 10% threshold, the payout for that cycle must be declined, even if the account is in profit. The account itself remains active for future payout cycles.
